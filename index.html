01 About

I am an economist and author specializing in behavioral finance and financial markets. My research combines behavioral perspectives with statistical analysis, machine learning, and natural experiments — asking how people actually make financial decisions, and what markets do with those decisions.

Before academia in the U.S., I directed the Research Department at Borsa Istanbul (the Istanbul Stock Exchange) and held faculty positions at several universities in the U.S. and Turkey, including the University of Texas at Dallas, Texas A&M Commerce, Rockford University, Bilgi University, and Ozyegin University.

I teach statistics, business finance, financial econometrics, macroeconomics, and real estate finance at the undergraduate and graduate levels, and I am a member of the American Economic Association.
