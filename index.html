01 About

Orhan Erdem is an economist and Clinical Associate Professor at the University of North Texas whose work lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, data science, and behavioral economics. His research applies rigorous quantitative methods to public policy, healthcare, and financial markets, turning complex data into actionable insights that support better decision-making.

Before joining the University of North Texas, he directed the Research Department at Borsa Istanbul (the Istanbul Stock Exchange) and held academic appointments in the United States and Turkey, including at the University of Texas at Dallas, Texas A&M University-Commerce, and Rockford University.
